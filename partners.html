Partner model

A clear split of responsibilities: we capture and package; partners provide judgement and sign-off when required.

Key principle: AIR MARINE does not act as a surveyor and does not issue technical certifications. Signed technical reporting is delivered by the partner.
AIR MARINE responsibilities
Evidence capture, structure and operational coordination.
  • Scope definition and capture planning.
  • 4K video / photos and optional live streaming.
  • Structured evidence packs, timelines and optional traceability modules.
  • Coordination with terminals/vessels and access procedures.
Partner responsibilities
Technical judgement and formal sign-off when required.
  • Inspection/supervision with technical criteria.
  • Issuance of technical reports (where applicable).
  • Certification/signature obligations under client contracts.
  • Integration of AIR MARINE evidence into formal documentation.

Surveyors / inspectors

Condition, damage, verification and claims support.

Marine Warranty Survey (MWS)

Critical operations, project cargo procedures and compliance contexts.

Local operational partners

Access, permitting, HSE interfaces and local response coverage.
